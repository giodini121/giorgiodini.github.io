About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Economics at the University of Bologna and a member of the ERC-funded Green Tipping project, led by Prof. Alessandro Tavoni.

I completed my PhD in Economics at the University of Bologna within the European Doctorate in Law and Economics (EDLE), a joint programme offered by the universities of Bologna, Rotterdam, and Hamburg.

My research examines human behaviour in the face of environmental and climate challenges, using methods from experimental economics, including laboratory, online, and field experiments.

Since 2024, I have been a member of the Youth Scholar Group of the Beijer Institute for Ecological Economics.

I have held visiting positions at Princeton University (2024) and Erasmus University Rotterdam (2021–2022).
